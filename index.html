Milu->In

It is Java based app that helps with the sorting and listing of soldiers . the app sends sms messages to the soldier on the day of his arrival and it can then read the sms inbox file to know whether he arrived or not . The app uses two SQLite DB that is created locally in your device one is for the details of the soldier, and another sets the list of all the invited soldiers .
